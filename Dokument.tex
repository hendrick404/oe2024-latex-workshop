\section{Quick Maths}

Seien \(x, y \in \mathbb{R}\). Es gilt
\[ |x+y| \leq |x| + |y|. \]

Sei \(z = (z_1, \dots, z_n) \in \mathbb{R}^n\).
Wir definieren die \(p\)-Norm
\begin{equation}
    ||z||_p = \left(\sum_{i=1}^n z_i^p\right)^\frac{1}{p}\label{eq:p-norm}
\end{equation}
